Upload:
Endpoint: /files/upload
curl -X POST -F "file=@path_to_your_file.sol" http://localhost:3000/files/upload

Extract:
Endpoint: /files/extract
sample request:
curl -X POST -H "Content-Type: application/json" -d "{\"filePath\": \"C:\\Users\\anish\\Downloads\\GBC_Blockchain_Development\\Blockhack\\smartcontract-analyzer\\uploads\\1698160952435-BridgeB.sol\"}" http://localhost:3000/files/extract

General Summary (after extracting data):
Endpoint: /analysis/general
curl -X POST http://localhost:3000/analysis/general -H "Content-Type: application/json"
